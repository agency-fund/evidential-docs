# 🕒 Experiment Results History

We’re excited to introduce **Experiment Results History** — a new feature that lets you explore how your experiment outcomes changed over time and view results exactly as they appeared on any given day.

______________________________________________________________________

## Why this matters

When teams make program or product decisions based on experiment results, it’s critical to preserve the context of those decisions.
With **Results History**, Evidential now serves as a true **experiment registry** — giving you a reliable record of what your data showed *at the time the decision was made*.

No more guesswork when reporting back to leadership or revisiting old experiments.

______________________________________________________________________

## What it does

- **Automatic multi-daily snapshots:** Evidential now takes regular snapshots of your experiment analysis.
- **Playback over time:** View how each metric or variation’s results evolved — complete with confidence intervals or probability distributions.
- **Historical views:** Select any date to see exactly what your team saw then, freezing the results in time.

______________________________________________________________________

## How to use it

You’ll find a new **time series view** in your experiment results page.
You'll also find a drowdown and interactive clicks that will bring you back to the details of a specific snapshots

______________________________________________________________________

## Why it’s useful

- Audit trail for evidence-based decisions
- Transparency in reporting and accountability
- Confidence in archiving and sharing experiment learnings

______________________________________________________________________

✨ With Experiment Results History, your data becomes a living record — helping you learn not just *what worked*, but *how and when you learned it*.
